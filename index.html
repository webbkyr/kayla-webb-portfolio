Bio

Hello! My name is Kayla. I’m a software developer who likes to build cool, responsive web applications using HTML5, CSS3, Javascript and NodeJS. I learned the MERN stack as a student in Thinkful's Engineering Immersion program, but I’m willing and excited to explore other technologies!
Before I was a developer, I worked in the federal legislative policy sphere. I caught the tech bug as a data analyst, where I collaborated with a team of analysts managing congressional and lobbying data. Writing SQL queries was my first taste of coding and I knew I wanted more. In my spare time, I train in weightlifting and compete in functional fitness competitions.
